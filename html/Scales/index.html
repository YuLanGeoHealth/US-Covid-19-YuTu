This map shows the bivariate map of relative risk of cluster and relative risk of regions at different scales, using the prospective space-time scan statistics. The value of two variables are ranged from lowest, low, medium to high. You can hover the region to get details. As an example, the data is ranged from December 1st 2021 to February 28th 2022.
